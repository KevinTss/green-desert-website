عن الشركة

Green Desert is a Saudi Arabian biotechnology startup founded in January 2020. We address the region’s most pressing environmental challenges through innovative, sustainable solutions rooted in Cannabis sativa (industrial hemp), aligned with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030.

We combine science, policy, and industry execution to prove hemp’s role in the region’s sustainability transition.

2020 • License secured

Green Desert founded and awarded the first Saudi license for industrial hemp research and deployment.

  • MEWA and SFDA approvals formalized the national industrial hemp research framework.
  • Internal governance, lab partnerships, and compliance reporting protocols were established.

2022 • Mega-project pilots

Signed proof-of-concept deployments with NEOM-aligned and Red Sea corridor developments.

  • Demonstrated structural and environmental KPIs within giga project testbeds across desert climates.
  • Codified HSSE and construction SOPs with multidisciplinary engineering and materials teams.

2024 • GCC expansion

Launched regional partnerships across GCC and initiated African feasibility studies with public-sector partners.

  • Signed GCC MoUs covering logistics, procurement, and bilingual deployment support.
  • Rolled out digital carbon-accounting dashboards and ministerial reporting kits.

2026 • Public-private delivery

Commissioning carbon-negative material packages for municipal housing, transport, and utilities programs in Riyadh and the Eastern Province.

  • Delivered turnkey material suites for municipal housing, transit, and utilities mandates.
  • Scaled training hubs so public-private delivery teams share unified playbooks.

2028 • Export-ready processing

Bringing an integrated agro-industrial campus online to supply certified hemp inputs across MENA and Africa with audited traceability.

  • Commissioned an integrated agro-industrial campus with accredited QA and traceability labs.
  • Activated bonded logistics corridors to serve MENA and African partners with certified biomaterials.

2030 • Vision 2030 outcomes

Fully embedded hemp circularity across giga projects, delivering local biomaterials, verified carbon credits, and future-proof jobs for the Green Saudi vision.

  • Achieved full hemp circularity across giga assets with independently verified carbon offsets.
  • Graduated Saudi suppliers and SMEs into the Green Saudi marketplace with long-term jobs.

Our Mission

We aim to break the vicious cycle of desertification, water scarcity, and soil degradation with practical, scalable solutions. Our biotechnology-driven products and services reduce waste, turn byproducts into value, and power a greener circular economy across the Kingdom.

We combine science, policy, and industry execution to prove hemp’s role in the region’s sustainability transition.

Our values

Science-led impact

عبدالهادي العامر

المؤسس

مؤسس الصحراء الخضراء. تعرّف عبدالهادي على نبات القنب — خصوصًا الاستخدام الطبي — عند إقرار تعديل كولورادو 64 في 6 نوفمبر 2012 الذي أدى إلى التقنين الترفيهي. بعد ذلك بوقت قصير أصبح رائد أعمال وبنى سمعته في بناء العلاقات ضمن الأعمال الخضراء حول العالم.
