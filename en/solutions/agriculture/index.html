Agriculture

Deploying climate-adapted crops and regenerative agronomy to secure the Kingdom’s industrial feedstock. We engineer high-yield, water-efficient systems that restore soil health and drive national resource autonomy.

Sustainable agriculture is a cornerstone of environmental resilience and economic diversification under Saudi Vision 2030. Our agricultural solutions focus on improving productivity while reducing pressure on water resources and soil systems. By integrating regenerative practices and climate-adapted crops, we support long-term agricultural viability in arid and semi-arid regions.

Our approach combines science-based agronomy with regional priorities: Assessment of soil quality and land capability, Crop selection suited for arid climates, Efficient water and nutrient management, Continuous performance monitoring and optimization. This ensures agricultural productivity while protecting natural resources.

Advancing the Kingdom’s food and material security through high-yield, water-efficient regenerative agronomy.

Arid-Climate Genetics

Seed varieties optimized for the Kingdom’s temperature extremes and high salinity levels.

Water-Efficiency Protocols

Advanced irrigation strategies designed to minimize agricultural water use.

Soil Health Restoration

Regenerative practices that rebuild organic matter and structural integrity in degraded lands.

Rapid Biomass Growth

High-yield cycles delivering consistent industrial raw materials in under 150 days.

Advanced Technology

Using precision a gritech and salt-tolerant crop genetics to maximize biomass yields while significantly reducing freshwater demand.

Sustainable Solutions

Applying soil restoration practices and efficient nutrient management to convert arid land into productive, carbon- sequestering agricultural systems.

Expert Support

Managing the full cultivation lifecycle—from land capability assessment to optimized harvesting—ensuring a reliable, locally secured feedstock supply.
