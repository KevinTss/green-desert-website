Waste & Remediation

Transforming industrial waste into valuable resources through phytoremediation and circular processing. We engineer natural systems for soil rehabilitation and land restoration to meet national environmental standards.

Waste & Remediation solutions address critical environmental challenges by transforming industrial and agricultural waste streams into high-value resources. Our approach leverages biological systems for soil detoxification and land rehabilitation, directly supporting Saudi Vision 2030 goals for environmental protection, circular economy development, and the restoration of the Kingdom’s natural ecosystems.

Environmental Assessment Conducting comprehensive site audits and soil/water analysis to identify contaminant profiles and establish baseline carbon sequestration metrics.

Phytoremediation Deployment Implementing targeted cultivation of specific hemp varieties engineered to extract heavy metals and pollutants while stabilizing the soil structure.

Circular Biomass Recovery Harvesting the remediation crop and utilizing specialized processing to safely sequester captured toxins or convert clean biomass into industrial feedstock.

Impact Validation & MRV Providing final technical reports, soil health certification, and verified carbon-negative data for national regulatory compliance and ESG reporting.

Transforming environmental liabilities into industrial assets through precision phytoremediation and waste recycling.

Soil Phytoremediation

Utilizing specialized hemp cultivars to extract heavy metals and toxins from degraded industrial soil.

Carbon Sequestration

Verified protocols for capturing atmospheric carbon and storing it permanently within the soil and processed biomass.

Circular Waste Systems

Converting agricultural by-products into high-performance industrial feedstock, eliminating landfill dependency.

Land Rehabilitation

Advanced biological treatments designed to restore fertility and structural integrity to arid or over-farmed land.

Effluent Bio Filtration

Engineering natural fiber systems to filter and remediate industrial wastewater before discharge.

Advanced Technology

Deploying hemp systems to remediate soils and process industrial residues into controlled biomass via precision monitoring and lab validation.

Sustainable Solutions

Converting organic waste and remediation biomass into safe, usable outputs like bioenergy feedstock and stabilized industrial inputs.

Expert Support

Delivering site audits, waste characterization, remediation protocols, and verified Monitoring, Reporting, and Verification (MRV).
