Construction

Creating low-carbon building envelopes to support the Kingdom’s urban growth. Our hempcrete systems are developed using verified mix designs and strict QA/QC protocols to meet the rigorous structural and thermal performance requirements of large-scale Saudi developments.

Hemp-based construction materials offer a low-carbon alternative to conventional building systems, combining high-performance insulation and durability. Engineered for arid and GCC climates, these materials reduce reliance on carbon-intensive inputs while supporting circular, climate-resilient construction at scale, By replacing traditional, energy heavy materials.

Our bio-composite solutions allow builders to significantly decrease the embodied carbon of their projects. This strategy not only meets Saudi Arabia's environmental thermal requirements but also provides a measurable pathway toward achieving national net-zero targets.

Bio-Mass Processing: Converting raw hemp stalks into high-quality hurd and fiber.

Precision Formulation: Utilizing our verified mix designs to create climate- adaptive binders.

Industrial Manufacturing: Scaling the production of blocks, panels, and spray-applied insulation.

Site Integration: Delivering ready-to-use building envelopes that meet Saudi building codes.
