Animal care

Providing high-performance bio-materials for livestock comfort and health management. We engineer traceable, absorbent, and antimicrobial solutions that enhance animal welfare and hygiene while optimizing operational biosecurity across the livestock sector.

Hemp-based animal care solutions provide clean, high-performance alternatives for livestock comfort, nutrition, and health management. We use bio-based inputs to enhance animal welfare and hygiene while reducing environmental impact.

Aligned with Saudi Vision 2030, our animal care portfolio supports sustainable food systems, responsible resource use, and improved agricultural efficiency. By deploying superior, locally sourced bio-materials, we ensure a sovereign supply of traceable, compostable solutions that drive the Kingdom’s circular bio-economy.

Material Analysis: Auditing livestock environments to determine the optimal fiber-to-core ratio for absorbency and ammonia control.

Local Sourcing: Processing locally grown biomass into dust-screened, antimicrobial bedding and nutritional feedstock. Deployment Support: Providing guidance on integration into existing farm management systems to improve biosecurity and animal health.

Waste Valorization: Facilitating the transition of used materials into nutrient-rich compost, returning value to the agricultural land.

Enhancing livestock biosecurity and welfare with antimicrobial, absorbent bio-materials for cleaner agricultural systems.

Superior Absorbency

Absorbs up to 4x its weight, maintaining a drier and cleaner environment.

Antimicrobial Performance

Natural resistance to bacteria, mold, and pests without chemical treatment.

Respiratory Protection

Dust-screened materials designed to minimize respiratory stress for animals and handlers.

Ammonia Control

High cation-exchange capacity that neutralizes odors and improves air quality in enclosures.

Thermal Insulation

Provides consistent temperature regulation for comfort in both extreme heat and cold.

Advanced Technology

Using the natural antimicrobial and high absorbency properties of hemp to create pathogen-resistant environments for commercial livestock.

Sustainable Solutions

Transforming industrial biomass into fully compostable bedding and nutrition inputs that reduce waste and return vital nutrients to the soil.

Expert Support

Providing Seed-to-Stable traceability and moisture- performance data to help producers meet high standards of animal welfare.
