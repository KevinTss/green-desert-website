Exclusive Partnership Between Green Desert Establishment and La Chanvrière

September 23, 2023ByGreen Desert

Partnership Overview

In a historic move to drive innovation and sustainability in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Green Desert Establishment has announced the signing of an exclusive partnership with La Chanvrière, a global leader in industrial hemp products. The contract signing ceremony took place in September 2023 in Paris, France, in the presence of top executives from both companies.

The event was attended by prominent figures who praised the vision of this partnership, aimed at advancing sustainability in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and beyond. This strategic alliance empowers Green Desert to deliver high-quality, innovative products based on industrial hemp, solidifying its position as a leader in sustainable biotechnology.

The partnership aligns with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030, bringing advanced technologies and sustainable products to support local and regional markets.
