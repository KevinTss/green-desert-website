## Why Riyadh

Our new headquarters in Riyadh positions Green Desert closer to public and private partners delivering Vision 2030 initiatives. The office will act as a hub for engineering, agronomy, and commercial teams supporting deployments across the Kingdom.

### What to expect

- Bilingual support for giga-project stakeholders
- Faster pilot setup and material validation
- Training programs for contractors and supply-chain partners
