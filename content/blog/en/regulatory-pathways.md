## Licenses and agencies

Industrial hemp deployment requires coordination with MEWA, SFDA, and local municipalities. Clear SOPs reduce project risk and speed approvals.

### Best practices

- Early engagement with authorities
- Transparent QA/QC documentation
- Training programs for contractors and operators
